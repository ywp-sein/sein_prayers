# Comprehend Your Love on the Cross 

This is the 40th weekly prayer:



Dear Heavenly Father,



Help us to believe that

Where we are will not define us,

What we’ve been through won’t confine us.

Chains of injustice can’t fine us,

Only the Way we walk will refine us. 



You are the Way, the Truth, the Life.

Help us to comprehend Your love on the cross more than anything else,

and walk in freedom and joy.



In Jesus’ precious and mighty name, I pray and receive.

Amen.
