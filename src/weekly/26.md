# Pray and Prayers

In the past few days, we are happy that we can pray and have Bible study together. We appreciate that God granted us the surprising chances to do that.



This is the 26th Weekly prayer:



Dear Lord, Heavenly Father,



We thank You that we can have these precious chances to pray together and study Your word together. May Your Word establish our hearts, and continue to humble us, so that we will have wise words for others, and have wisdom for the people in need.



In You we live and move and have our being, please give us the heart to continue to pursue You, and always remind us of Your holy Love, grace and mercy, reveal more Yourself to us, so that we may be more and more like You, because we will know Who You are deeper and deeper.



We also want to pray for all the homeless people in Berlin right now. We ask for Your mercy that please show them Your warmth, and please encounter them and invite them to start a personal relationship with You, just as You did to us before. 



We praise You, dear Lord. May the grace of the Lord Jesus Christ, the love of God, the communion of the Holy Spirit be with us, forever and ever. May You use us to bless this city, and as the preparation for the bigger prayer house to be built.



In the holy name of our Lord Jesus Christ we pray,

Amen
