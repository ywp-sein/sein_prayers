# 47 Sing New Songs

*22.04.2026*

*"Believing, we rejoice*<br>
*To see the curse remove;*<br>
*We bless the Lamb with cheerful voice,*<br>
*And sing His bleeding love"*

---Isaac Watts, *Not all the Bood of Beasts*



This is the 47th weekly prayer:



Dear Heavenly Father,



Thank You for Your mercy and grace, for You patiently wait for more vessels to be emptied by You and filled with Your glory.



Help us to believe and rejoice, to wait and see curses removed in this city. I ask You to give more cheerful and united voices to this city, to sing of Your bleeding love. Renew Your children's minds daily, so that we are able to sing new songs to praise You.



Thank You for Your unfailing and unconditional love; may everything that has breath praise You.



Hallowed be Thy name.



In Jesus’ precious and mighty name, I pray and receive.

Amen.
