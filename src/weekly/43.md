# 43 Integrity

*25.03.2026*

God created the world through His Word. Whatever He says will come to pass. He is faithful to His Word. Integrity is something that is greatly lacking in this society. 



This is my 43rd weekly prayer,



Dear Heavenly Father,



Please continue to teach me how to align my words with my deeds. Please teach me how to always speak and act according to the measure of faith You have assigned to me. And Lord, please continue to increase my faith in You, so that I may be able to guide more people to You through You in the future—not by winning arguments in debates, but by pointing out their broken reality and guiding them to You with hope.



For Your name’s sake, dear Lord, as You have prompted me to post prayers in public, I ask You to show Your faithfulness through these prayers.



You are the faithful One who calls me, and You will do it. For I am Your workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which You prepared beforehand, that I should walk in them through Your Holy Spirit.



This is Your promise. All glory to the Holy One.



In Jesus’ precious and mighty name, I pray and receive.

 Amen.
