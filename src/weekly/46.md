# 46 Narrow

*15.04.2026*

Last week I shared with a sister that if we think the way is wide, then we will search for the wide way; if we think the way is narrow, then we will search for the narrow way. And Christ is indeed that narrow and only door.



This is the 46th weekly prayer,



Dear Heavenly Father,



Help us to know the Truth, to know more of You. Don't let us put You in the box, don't let us put You in our narrow thoughts and be astray in the wide road that lead to destruction, but help us to get through the narrow door, please lead us to abundant life. 



Lead us not into any comfort that is not out of You, but only to receive Your comfort through the faith given by You, to receive Your comfort by believing Your Spirit is praying for us, believing our names are written in Your hands and the book of life.



Dear Lord, thank You for the abundance that You are willing to give us in the narrow path.
