# 37 Humor and Joy

*11.02.2026*

In the past few days, God has brought me several fun and surprising encounters. He knows that I enjoy interesting and unexpected things. I asked Him to speak to me in humorous ways, and He continues to do so. I am very happy. 



This is the 37th weekly prayer:



Dear Heavenly Father,



Thank You for being a humorous Daddy! Even when Your humor sometimes feels like a horror to others, it is good, because it reveals what they truly fear and the lies they cling to. Thank You for the trust I have in You, which allows me to experience more and more joy in my abundant life, together with true brothers and sisters who pursue You.



May those who love You in this city enjoy more of Your grace and understand more of Your humor, so we can share with others that we have a loving and humorous Daddy. At the same time, dear Father, please let those who reject Your love encounter the consequences of their choices, so that they may eventually turn to You and be blessed with the beginning of understanding Your ways.



Dear Father, I pray that more people in this city will:

see Your humor in dealing with rumors,

hear truth instead of whispers,

speak life instead of suspicion,

choose the Gospel instead of gossip,

and walk in the light instead of echo chambers.



And when You speak, let us be silent to listen and learn the way You communicate, so that we too may eventually speak like You—even in a humorous way!



In Jesus’ precious and mighty name I pray and receive,

 Amen.
