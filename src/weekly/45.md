# Talents 

Capability is not the calling,

God's calling carries the capability He gives.



This is the 45th weekly prayer,



Dear Heavenly Father,



Thank You for the wilderness.



Thank You for the wilderness, where I can know more of You and come to know more of myself.



Thank You for the talents You have given me, especially those formed in the wilderness. I ask that You direct me to use them according to Your will. And unless I am filled with Your Spirit—unless I first seek and find Your will—please do not let me use them at all.



As You did with Bezalel in the wilderness, I believe You will do the same for me, granting me the ability to carry out and create all that You command me to do. Thank You for these callings. 



I ask that You would also do the same for those who will work with me in the future—that they would have confidence from You to use the talents You have given them to be used for Your will.



I trust You and I love You.



In Jesus’ precious and mighty name, I pray and receive.

Amen.
